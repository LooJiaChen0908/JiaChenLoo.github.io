Oreo® Cookie Gourmet Pudding Shots

Pudding shots are not just for college.

Ingredients

1 cup cold milk

¾ cup cake-flavored vodka (such as UV®)

1 (4 ounce) package cookies and cream instant pudding mix (such as Jell-O® Oreo®)

1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed

  DIRECTIONS

    Step 1
  • Stir milk, vodka, and pudding mix together in a bowl until mixture thickens. Fold in whipped topping. Spoon into disposable shot cups and refrigerate until chilled, at least 30 minutes.

    • Step 2
  • Cover bowl with plastic wrap and steep at room temperature for 18 to 24 hours.

    • Step 3
  • Line a strainer with several layers of cheesecloth and place atop a pitcher; strain coffee through the cheesecloth into the pitcher. Discard the grounds. To get a clearer brew, strain coffee again through coffee filters. Store in refrigerator.
